About Court Kings HQ

Court Kings HQ is a modern, automated hub for basketball stat fans. We use custom bots to track and post the most relevant, advanced, and nightly-changing NBA stats — built for fans who live for the numbers.

What We Do

Every night, our bots analyze NBA performances and deliver standout metrics — from clutch scoring to efficiency monsters, defensive beasts, and more. Our goal is to bring key insights to the forefront with zero fluff and maximum impact.

Our Bot Environments

🏀 Court Kings HQ: Regular Season

  • 🔥 Nightly Stats Bot – Tracks nightly leaders in points, assists, rebounds, and threes.
  • ⚡ Efficiency Bot – Highlights efficient scoring, plus-minus stars, defense, and triple-doubles.
  • ⏱️ Clutch Time Bot – Focuses on 4th quarter stats: points, FG%, assists, and best team finishes.
  • 👑 Season Scoring Leader Bot – Tracks top scorers all season by PPG and total points.
  • 📅 Calendar Bot – Posts each day’s NBA schedule.
  • ⏳ Milestone Watch Bot – Monitors players nearing major career milestones in certain statistics.

⚔️ Court Kings HQ: Playoffs Edition

  • 📅 Calendar Bot – Posts each day’s NBA playoff schedule.
  • 📊 Game Results Bot – Tweets final scores and series updates live during games.
  • 🏆 Series Royalty Bot – Announces completed series and top player performances.
  • 🔥 Playoff Points Leader Bot – Tracks the current leading scorer of the postseason.
  • 📜 On This Day Bot – Shares a historic NBA playoff moment tied to today’s date.
  • 🎯 Assist Leaders Bot – Tracks top assist leaders in the playoffs.
  • 💪 Rebound Leaders Bot – Tracks top rebounders in all playoff games.
  • 🛡️ Steal Leaders Bot – Highlights players leading in playoff steals.
  • 🧱 Block Leaders Bot – Highlights players leading in playoff blocks.
  • 👑 Spotlight Bot – Features a standout playoff performer.
